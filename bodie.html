Bodie State Historic Park

Bodie State Historic Park is a fascinating ghost town located in the Eastern Sierra region of California. Step back in time and explore the well-preserved remnants of a once-booming mining town.

Stroll through the deserted streets and buildings, and imagine life during the town's gold rush era. Learn about the history and stories of the people who lived and worked in Bodie. The town provides a unique glimpse into the past with its preserved buildings, including a schoolhouse, church, saloons, and homes.

As you explore Bodie, you'll be captivated by the eerie atmosphere and the sense of stepping into a bygone era. The park offers guided tours and interpretive displays that provide insights into the town's history and its significance as a California State Historic Park.
