Explore California's Cultural Riches

San Francisco's Chinatown

Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of San Francisco's Chinatown, one of the oldest and largest Chinatowns in North America. Explore its bustling streets, visit ornate temples, savor authentic Chinese cuisine, and shop for traditional crafts.

Learn More

Getty Center, Los Angeles

Discover world-class art and culture at the Getty Center in Los Angeles. Explore its impressive collection of European paintings, sculptures, and decorative arts, and enjoy breathtaking views of the city. The architectural masterpiece itself is a sight to behold.

Learn More

Golden Gate Park, San Francisco

Experience the cultural oasis of Golden Gate Park in San Francisco. Explore its lush gardens, visit world-class museums such as the de Young Museum and California Academy of Sciences, and enjoy outdoor concerts and events.

Learn More

Hollywood Walk of Fame, Los Angeles

Walk in the footsteps of stars along the Hollywood Walk of Fame in Los Angeles. Admire the iconic stars embedded in the sidewalk, visit the TCL Chinese Theatre, and explore the vibrant entertainment district of Hollywood.
